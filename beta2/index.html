Who We Are

We are a bunch of IIT grads passionate about teaching and helping our students reach their full potential. We have moulded an effective and proven teaching formula from our own experiences while tackling various competitive exams like NET / GATE / JEST / JAM. At Bhauthik, our mentors are proactive researchers from all around the globe, who are also highly skilled at the art of teaching. Don't worry; we are here to help you cultivate this habit of smart-learning through our uniquely devised strategies. And you get all these benefits without burning a hole in your pockets!

Dr.Anu Babusenan

Anu is a passionate researcher and teacher with more than 7 years of experience in Graduate level teaching as well as in research became one of the founding members of Bhauthik family. Currently, Anu is a research associate on next-generation AMOLED displays at MEMS, Department of Electrical Engineering, IIT Madras. In 2020, she finished her PhD in Physics from Department of Physics, IIT Madras. She is specialized in spectroscopic studies of organic semiconductors. She is an expert in building time resolved spectroscopic experimental set ups. During her research at IITM, she also worked as a teaching assistant for MSc and BTech students. Anu is a university first rank holder and has cleared the competitive exams CSIR NET, JEST and GATE with top rank. She has developed very novel and effective methods to improve the problem-solving skills which will help her students to crack the exams smartly and quickly. She is also a science writer and science correspondent in leading Malayalam newspapers, with the pen name ‘Anu B. Karingannoor’ and known for writing Physics behind everything to layman in very simple language

Suraj TS

Suraj is one of the founders of Bhauthik academy of Physics, which was his brainchild with the vision to impart knowledge to reach the graduate level students and to encourage them in taking up research as a career. He is an active researcher in the field of spintronics, magnetism and superconductivity. Currently, he is a senior research fellow in the Dept of Physics, IIT Madras. During his research career, he was a prestigious DAAD fellow in Walther Meissner Institute in Technical University of Munich (TUM) Germany. In addition, he was also a visiting researcher in NUSNNI group headed by Prof T. Venkatesan (CEO, Neocera) at the National University of Singapore (NUS). Suraj was also a JASSO fellow in Shibaura Institute of Technology, Tokyo. Suraj's research interest includes spin current generation, detection and transmission using electrical and microwave techniques in strongly correlated systems. Before joining for PhD at IITM, he was a lecturer in Amrita school of arts and sciences. He has a track record of qualifying CSIR JRF and GATE exams with top ranks. From his vast experience in teaching and research, his lectures will merge the current research directions in solid-state physics with the elementary concepts.

Dr.Dileep Kottilil

Being in love with teaching, Dr Dileep became one of the four founders of Bhauthik online family. In 2013, he completed his MSc Physics from IIT Madras during which he developed his passion for computational and experimental physics. Later, he was selected for the joint-PhD, a prestigious doctoral research program conducted by both IIT Madras and National University of Singapore (NUS). His PhD work is about the discovery of the existence of polaritons (special quantum particles) in an artificial material called Metal-Organic Framework (MOF) thereby introducing a new family of materials (MOFs) to the scientific community for the applications in the field of strong light-matter coupling. His unique teaching style with his-own physics simulations make students understand the confusing topics like relativity in a matter of minutes. He is also a proactive science blogger, machine learning programmer, optical-system consultant, software developer and freelance cover-art designer.
